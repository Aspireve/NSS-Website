TCET NSS UNIT

Not me but You

Who are we

TCET NSS UNIT is an extra-curricular activity aided by the University of Mumbai and the Department of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India. Holding the motto NOT ME BUT YOU, it aims enhancing the personality of volunteers and promote self-less service for the well-being of society. TCET NSS UNIT was established in 2005 with 42 members and has grown to a strength of 200 members, with over 1350 students enrolled till date.

What we do

The TCET NSS Unit is dedicated to spreading joy through community service, emphasizing education and advancement as our primary goals. Our inventive initiatives across departments cater to the entire campus community, crafting cherished experiences. Activities encompass tree planting, voter ID registration, e-waste drives, blood donation camps, medical check-ups, disaster management, national integration camps, and adventurous programs.

Our Achievements

Our exceptional achievements are a direct result of the remarkable teamwork and relentless commitment of our NSS volunteers. Working harmoniously together, they have consistently contributed to positive change, making a meaningful impact on the world. Their collective efforts embody the spirit of unity, transforming challenges into opportunities for creating a better and more compassionate global community.

Our Objectives

Our primary objective is to address pressing issues and create lasting change in the communities we serve. In every project, we strategically design initiatives that not only target specific goals but also aim to fulfill multiple objectives simultaneously. This holistic approach ensures that each endeavor contributes meaningfully to our overarching mission, fostering comprehensive and sustainable impact.

Community Understanding

The objective ensures that the volunteers gain a profound understanding of the community in which they work.

Assess and Engage

The second objective focuses on identifying the specific needs and challenges faced by the community in which they work.

Competence Development

Our third objective is to equip volunteers with the competence needed for effective group-living and the ability to share responsibilities.

Leadership and Democracy

The fourth objective is selected with an aim to cultivate leadership qualities and democratic attitudes among all the volunteers.
