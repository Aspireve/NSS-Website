Community Understanding

Fostering In-Depth Knowledge

The first objective is to help volunteers gain a profound understanding of the community in which they work. This involves immersing themselves in the community's culture, values, and unique characteristics.

Assessment and Engagement

Collaborative Problem Solving

The second objective focuses on identifying the specific needs and challenges faced by the community. Volunteers are encouraged to engage with community members to collectively find solutions and address these issues.

Competence Development

Group-Living Skills

Our third objective is to equip volunteers with the competence needed for effective group-living and the ability to share responsibilities within the community. This involves teamwork, communication, and cooperation.

Leadership and Democracy

Nurturing Leadership Qualities

The fourth objective aims to cultivate leadership qualities and democratic attitudes among volunteers. They will learn to lead by example, encourage inclusivity, and promote democratic decision-making processes within the community.
